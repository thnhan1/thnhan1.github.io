It's hard to make a saw that cuts trees but not arms.
<!--more-->

This is a metaphor about creating tools that are powerful enough to be useful but safe enough to not cause harm. The balance between functionality and safety is something every tool maker must consider.

When we design tools, whether they're physical or digital, we're always walking this line. We want our tools to be effective at their intended purpose, but we also need to consider the potential for misuse or accidental harm.

The best tools are those that understand their purpose deeply and excel at it, while making it difficult to use them in harmful ways.
