Phở Bò: A traditional Vietnamese beef noodle soup known for its fragrant broth and hearty noodles.

Bánh Mì: A Vietnamese sandwich that is a product of French colonialism in Indochina, combining ingredients from the French (baguettes, pâté, jalapeño, and mayonnaise) with native Vietnamese ingredients, such as cilantro, cucumber, and pickled carrots and daikon.

Gỏi Cuốn: Vietnamese spring roll typically featuring pork, shrimp, herbs, rice vermicelli, and other ingredients wrapped in rice paper.
